Firm Overview

Beverly Hills DUI Attorney A Former Prosecutor, an Accomplished Defense Attorney

The Law Offices of Jonathan Franklin is a dynamic law firm providing aggressive criminal defense to clients in the Los Angeles area and throughout Southern California. Contact us now at 310.273.9600 to schedule your free consultation.

Beverly Hills Criminal Lawyer Jonathan Franklin interned with the public defender’s office while in law school, then first practiced criminal law as a prosecutor upon graduation. He now uses his knowledge and skill to defend people accused of serious felony and misdemeanor crimes, including DUI, drug charges, white collar crimes, domestic violence, lewd conduct, and others.

After graduating from Florida’s oldest law school, Stetson University College of Law in St. Petersburg, Florida, Mr. Franklin joined the Orange County State Attorney’s office in Orlando. As an assistant state attorney, he prosecuted a wide range of criminal cases. He relocated to Southern California in 1993, where he worked for Spelling Television, Inc., Dick Clark Productions, Inc., and in private practice.

When deciding to start his own practice, Mr. Franklin was determined to create a law firm that was dedicated to helping clients face and overcome difficult legal problems while providing them with the personal service they deserve. Thus, the Law Offices of Jonathan Franklin was created exclusively to handle criminal defense matters.
